--- lang: ja ENTRYTYPE: article ID: 2018ApJ...859...29N abstract: Using two-dimensional magnetohydrodynamic simulations, we investigated the gas dynamics around a black hole (BH) plunging into a molecular cloud. In these calculations, we assumed a parallel- magnetic-field layer in the cloud. The size of the accelerated region is far larger than the Bondi-Hoyle-Lyttleton radius, being approximately inversely proportional to the Alfvén Mach number for the plunging BH. Our results successfully reproduce the “Y” shape in position-velocity maps of the “Bullet” in the W44 molecular cloud. The size of the Bullet is also reproduced within an order of magnitude using a reasonable parameter set.
